Enemies

On the map there are a certain number of enemies that will hinder you to get to the gate

Each enemy has its own characteristics(damage, hp, vampirism)

They can kill you!!!

Moving

To move the hero press one of the control keys

Attack

To kill the enemy you just need for him to go!

Press the right arrow to kill the enemy

Unusual chests

If you killed a chest you can drop:

  • 0 - 2 hillpoint
  • 0 - 2 damage
  • 0 - 1 vampirism

Vampires

If you kill a vampire, you will get 1 to vampirism!

Random game

If you start a Random game, then the map and characters will be generated randomly

Level game

If you start a Level game, then each level has its own map and characters

Good luck!
